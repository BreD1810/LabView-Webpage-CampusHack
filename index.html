CampusHack2018 - Team Kirkstars - LabView

Our app idea for CampusHack2018 was "LabView".

As I am sure you will agree, walking up 3 whole flights of stairs is the worst when you get to the top to realise that all of the machines are in use. How nice would it be to have a webpage that displays the all of the machines along with on/off states?

Well now there is!!! (or hopefully in the future once it's actually implemented!)
